About {{site.title}}

ThingLearn was born from discussions with teachers and education professionals about their interest in the Internet of Things and a lack of curriculum to help students learn.

Built by Jen Looper, a developer, and Qing Hu, a professor at MIT, ThingLearn is all about easing the path for students to learn innovative tools and technologies.

This site includes beginning, intermediate, and advanced projects that will help students discover the many things that they can create using IoT devices.

In the process, they are encouraged to learn programming basics and expand their knowledge of systems design and signal processing.

Visit the Essentials section to learn about the basic concepts taught by each project, such as basic circuit-building and how electricity works.

You can also purchase parts for each project via links at the top of each project page.

ThingLearn is proud to partner with Hackster.io who hosts our projects.
